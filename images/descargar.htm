Palma de Mallorca

Everybody finds something to their liking on Palma de Mallorca, the largest of the Balearic Islands. Whether you are looking for peace and quiet or an active holiday full of fun, this is the place to be. Let yourself be enchanted by one of the gems of the Mediterranean and book flights to Mallorca now!

Why book a flight from the Antwerp International Airport to Palma de Mallorca?

• Charming bays and child-friendly beaches in the east
• A vibrant nightlife at El Arenal and Playa de Palma for anyone looking for fun and entertainment
• The Sierra de Tramuntana in the north, the most beautiful mountain villages being Deià and Valldemossa
• A must for every visitor to Mallorca: the capital and mini-metropolis of Palma de Mallorca
